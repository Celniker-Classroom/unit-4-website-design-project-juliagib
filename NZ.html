New Zealand


New Zealand is in the Southwest Pacific Ocean, just southeast of Australia. It is made up of two main islands and many smaller ones. New Zealand is famous for its beautiful lanscapes that include glaciers, lakes, beaches, and even enourmas and beautiful mountains. It is home to so many unique animals like the Kiwi bird, a flightless bird that only lives in New Zealand. New Zealand is a place of stunning natural beauty.

Fun Facts about New Zealand:

  1. The people there are called Kiwis!
  2. Many scenes from The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit were filmed in New Zealand, and you can even visit a few of the filming locations!
  3. Originally, New Zealand has almost no land mammals, just birds
  4. New Zealand has glowworm caves that tourists can visit
